Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 26, 2026

Summary: This extension is local-first. Your data never leaves your browser unless you explicitly trigger the AI scheduling feature, in which case data is sent directly to your chosen AI provider (e.g., OpenAI). We do not own any servers and do not collect any analytics.

1. Data Collection and Storage

All project data, resource lists, and scheduling results are stored exclusively in your browser's IndexedDB and local storage. We have no access to this data.

2. AI Integration

When using the "AI Scheduling" feature, the extension sends your project and resource descriptions to the AI API endpoint you have configured. This data is handled by the API provider (e.g., OpenAI) according to their own privacy policy.

3. Permissions

  • Storage: Required to save your settings and project data locally.
  • Host Permissions: Used solely to display resource alerts on Jira pages. No Jira data is harvested or sent to external parties.

4. Zero Tracking

We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any form of analytics. Your privacy is protected by design.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
